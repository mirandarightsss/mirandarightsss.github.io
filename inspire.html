Jim Henson

James Henson was the main creative mind behind the Muppet Show, Sesame Street, Labyrinth, The Dark Crystal, and many other integral children's shows throughout the decades.

While he may not be the most influential or revolutionary man, he brought joy and learning to children worldwide and certainly made the world a better place through his creative genius.
